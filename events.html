Event Types

What We Offer

🎭
Student Concerts
Bi-annual recitals where every student performs the repertoire they've developed over the term. A proud milestone for families and students alike.
🎪
Cultural Festivals
Zangouleh students represent Persian musical heritage at major cultural festivals including Tirgan — Canada's largest Persian arts celebration.
🎓
Masterclasses
Workshops led by visiting master musicians from Iran and around the world. An invaluable window into authentic Persian classical tradition.
🛠️
Workshops
Focused skill workshops on specific topics — rhythm, improvisation, music theory, Music Wellness — open to students at all levels.
✍️
Composition Showcases
Original student compositions performed live. Celebrating creative voices and the musical ideas born in our Composition & Songwriting program.
📸
Photo & Video
All major events are professionally photographed and recorded. Students and families receive access to the media archive after each event.
