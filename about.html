Our Story

About Zangouleh

Who We Are

درباره زنگوله

Founded in 2011, Zangouleh Music School was built on a vision to preserve Persian musical heritage while embracing the full spectrum of world music. We are more than a school — we are a cultural home.

Our Educational Philosophy

We believe every student carries a unique musical voice waiting to be discovered. Our approach combines rigorous technique with artistic freedom — taught exclusively by professional instructors with real performance experience.

  • Only professional, performance-experienced instructors
  • Participation in major cultural festivals including Tirgan Festival
  • Rich cultural activities and community concerts
  • Both Iranian classical and Western music traditions
  • Music Wellness & therapy programs unique to Zangouleh
  • Professional recording studio on premises
  • Student portal for tracking progress and attendance

Timeline · سیر تاریخی

2011
Zangouleh Music School founded in Toronto — beginning a journey of musical excellence rooted in Persian heritage.
2013
Expanded instrument offerings to include Western instruments — Piano, Guitar, Violin, Drums, and Voice.
2014
First annual student concert — a landmark moment establishing our performance tradition.
2016
Participation in Tirgan Festival, connecting students to the broader Iranian diaspora community in Toronto.
2018
Music Wellness Program launched — expanding our mission beyond performance into healing through music.
2020
Recording Studio opened on premises, giving students professional-grade production experience.
2022
Student & Teacher Portal introduced — bringing digital tools for tracking sessions, attendance, and certificates.
2025
Celebrating 14 years — stronger than ever with expanded programs and a thriving community of musicians.

Cultural Presence

Beyond the Classroom

🎪
Tirgan Festival
Zangouleh students perform annually at Tirgan, one of the world's largest Persian arts and cultural festivals, held at Harbourfront Centre in Toronto.
🎓
Master Classes
We regularly host guest master musicians — giving students direct access to world-class performers and educators beyond our resident faculty.
🤝
Community
From student recitals to community workshops, Zangouleh is deeply woven into Toronto's Iranian diaspora and broader multicultural music scene.
