Policies & Terms

Clear, fair policies designed to protect students and ensure the best possible experience at Zangouleh.

01
Consultation
A meeting with one of our instructors before enrollment. There is no charge for the consultation — it is a conversation, not a lesson.
  • Complimentary — no obligation
  • In person, phone, or WhatsApp
  • Instrument & program guidance
  • Schedule via 647-866-7912
02
4-Session Package
An introductory package for new students wanting to try a program before committing to a full term. Ideal for beginners or students switching instruments.
  • 4 private or group lessons
  • Valid for 6 weeks from start date
  • Paid in full at registration
  • Non-transferable between students
03
12-Session Term
The standard Zangouleh term — 12 sessions of structured instruction, ending with your first live student concert performance.
  • 12 private or group lessons
  • Valid for 16 weeks from start date
  • 50% deposit at registration
  • Balance due before session 6
04
Lesson Hold Policy
Students may place enrollment on hold for medical or personal reasons without losing paid sessions.
  • Maximum 4-week hold per term
  • Request at least 48 hours in advance
  • Medical holds up to 8 weeks with documentation
  • Sessions do not expire during approved hold
05
Cancellation Policy
We respect your time and ask that you respect ours. Instructors are compensated for their scheduled time.
  • ≥ 24 hours notice: rescheduled at no charge
  • < 24 hours notice: session charged as attended
  • No-show: session charged in full
  • 3 no-shows may result in enrolment termination
06
Refund Policy
Refund eligibility decreases as the term progresses. Sessions may be transferred to another student in lieu of a refund.
  • Before session 1: full refund (less admin fee)
  • After sessions 1–3: 75% refund
  • After sessions 4–6: 50% refund
  • After session 7+: no refund — transfer only
