Find Your Instrument

From the ancient strings of Persia to the modern concert stage — every instrument, every tradition. Each instrument page includes levels, age recommendations, and instructor information.

Iranian Instruments · سازهای ایرانی

🎸
Tar · تار
The soul of Persian classical music. A long-necked lute with six strings and a rich, resonant voice. Central to the radif tradition and Persian classical repertoire.
BeginnerIntermediateAdvanced
🪕
Setar · سه‌تار
Delicate and intimate. The three-stringed setar produces a uniquely poetic sound prized in classical Persian music. Ideal for expressive, meditative playing.
BeginnerIntermediate
🎵
Santur · سنتور
A hammered dulcimer that creates shimmering, bell-like tones — the heartbeat of the Persian classical ensemble. Played with two mallets on 72 strings.
All Levels
🥁
Daf · دف
The sacred frame drum used in Sufi ceremonies and modern Persian music. The Daf is both spiritually profound and rhythmically powerful.
BeginnerIntermediate
🪘
Tombak · تنبک
The principal percussion instrument of Persian classical music. Complex rhythmic cycles mastered through focused, dedicated practice with a professional.
All Levels
